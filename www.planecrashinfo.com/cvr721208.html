December 8, 1972
Chicago, Illinois
United Air Lines, Flight 553
Boeing B-737-222
N9031U

The aircraft crashed while making a non-precision instrument approach to Runway 31L. The
accident occurred in a residential area approximately 1.5 miles southeast of the approach end
of Runway 31L. Upon reaching minimum descent altitude, the pilot raised the nose of the
aircraft to stop the descent. The stall warning horn sounded and continued to sound until the
plane crashed 17 seconds later. The aircraft was destroyed by impact and subsequent fire.
Forty-three of 61 aboard killed.
