September 1, 1983
  Near Sakhalin Island, Russia
Korean Airlines, Flight 007
Boeing B-747-230B
HL-7442

 On a flight from Alaska to South Korea, the airliner drifted off course and twice penetrated Soviet airspace. During the second penetration, the airliner was shot down by a Russian Su-15 Air Force fighter with air-to-air missiles. The aircraft crashed into international waters in the Sea of Japan. U.S. All 269 aboard killed.
