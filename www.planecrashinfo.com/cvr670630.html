June 30, 1967
Kai Tak, Hong Kong
Thai International, Flight 601
Sud Aviation SE-210 Caravelle III
HS-TGI

Ditched into the sea while on approach during a typhoon. Crew did not follow proper
approach procedures. The captain did not monitor the approach properly and the
co-pilot mishandled the aircraft after descending below minimum altitude. Twenty-four
of 80 aboard killed.
